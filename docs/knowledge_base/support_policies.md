# Support Knowledge Base

## Return Policy

Customers may request a return within 30 calendar days after delivery for eligible physical products. Returned items should be unused, undamaged, and include original accessories, manuals, and packaging when reasonably possible.

For cross-border orders, the support team should first confirm the order number, delivery date, destination country, product condition, and reason for return. If the return is due to a verified product defect, wrong item, or shipping damage, the merchant may provide a prepaid return label or reimburse reasonable return shipping after approval.

Return eligibility exclusions include final-sale products, heavily used items, missing serial-number products, hygiene-sensitive opened items, and products damaged by misuse. When an exclusion may apply, escalate to a support lead before denying the request.

Refunds should be issued to the original payment method after the returned item is received and inspected. Typical refund processing time is 5 to 10 business days after inspection, plus any bank or payment-provider processing time.

## Exchange Policy

Exchanges are available when replacement inventory exists in the customer's destination region. If inventory is unavailable, offer a refund, store credit, or a comparable alternative product where appropriate.

## Shipping And Delivery Policy

Standard international delivery times vary by destination, customs processing, carrier capacity, and local holidays. Support should avoid guaranteeing an exact delivery date unless the carrier has confirmed it.

For delayed cross-border orders, support should check carrier tracking, customs status, warehouse fulfillment status, and any regional disruption notices. If the shipment is needed for a launch or time-sensitive event, offer expedited replacement options only after confirming inventory and carrier availability.

## Compensation Guidance

For material shipping delays, wrong items, verified defects, or missed expedited-shipping commitments, support may offer one of the following remedies depending on severity: shipping-fee refund, replacement shipment, partial discount, store credit, or full refund after return approval.

Compensation should be documented in the ticket with the reason, amount, currency, approver if required, and customer-facing explanation.

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to a support lead when the customer requests compensation above the standard policy, threatens legal or chargeback action, reports safety issues, has a high-value bulk order, or needs an exception to return eligibility.
