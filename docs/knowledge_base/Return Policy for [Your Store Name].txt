Customs and Duties: For international returns, buyers are responsible for any customs fees, import duties, or taxes incurred during the return process. We cannot cover these charges.
Shipping Method: We recommend using a trackable shipping service or purchasing shipping insurance when returning items from overseas, as we cannot guarantee that we will receive your returned item.
